Detailed Tour Guide To Kannur


  • For people having an eye for the culture and wanting to know more about the country, Kannur is a must visit
  • Arakkal Museum highlights Kerala's one Muslim royal family.
  • The palm-fringed sands of Payyambalam Beach run along Kannur’s western shore

How to reach


The nearest airport to Kannur is the Calicut International Airport, Kozhikode, located at a distance of about 85 km from the town. Many flights from all over the country, including Mumbai, Delhi, Mangalore, Hyderabad and Chennai fly down to the Calicut International Airport It has its own Railway Station named as Kannur Railway Station. It is well connected to the major cities
