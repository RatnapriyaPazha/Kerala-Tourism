Detailed Tour Guide To Idukki


  • Idukki is yet another gorgeous hill station in Kerala
  • Situated near the Western Ghats and bordered by the hills
  • Located in the magical Western Ghats, this serene abode is nestled amidst lush and evergreen settings, and is a true treasure trove for the hill stations lovers on a Kerala trip
  • Munnar is the leading hill station, familiar to almost everyone visiting Kerala

How to reach


The nearest railway station is in Kottayam tucked 114 km away from the district.All the trains from major cities in India and the state have stopovers at Kottayam.. The closest airport is in Cochin which is about 98 km away. Taxis and buses are easily available from Cochin to Idukki.
