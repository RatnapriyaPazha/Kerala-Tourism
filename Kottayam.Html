Detailed Tour Guide To Kottayam


  • Kottayam is also called as “Akshara Nagari” which means the “city of letters”
  • Kottayam Town is the first town in India to have achieved 100% literacy
  • Flanked by the Western Ghats on the east and the Vembanad Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west, Kottayam

How to reach


State buses of Kerala Road Transport Corporation connect Kottayam to all other important cities of the state of Kerala. Kottayam is connected by bus to all the main cities. Kumarakom (13 km), Alleppey (46 km), Kochi (64 km) and Thekaddy (108 km) are the places near Kottayam. The Kottayam railway station located within the town resolves the most vital issue of the travelers of how to reach Kottayam. It connects with various cities of Kerala as well as the country.

Place to visit


Kottayam district comprises six municipal towns: Kottayam, Changanassery, Pala, Erattupetta, Ettumanoor, and Vaikom.The top attractions to visit in Kottayam are: Illikkal Kallu Elaveezhapoonchira Vaikom Mahadeva Temple Marmala Waterfall Thirunakkara Mahadeva Temple

Things to do


Kottayam is blessed with some of the beautiful sceneries, hill stations at just a stone throw distance away, temples, churches, mosques, backwaters, and of course the mighty waterfalls. The Western Ghats are undoubtedly the best in India and with that, when you have lots of green beauty and the stunning plantations like coconut, palm, spices, and rubber. You will fall in love with the immediate sight you set at them.

Ideal Time


Summer (March to May)Summers are a good time to visit the city, as summer comes with its own features, like special dishes from the cuisine, with the climatic conditions enhancing the experience of visiting certain destinations, or the fests or exhibitions that happen annually around this time,Monsoon (June to August),Winter (December to February)
